Soul Searcher is a turn-based strategy game with procedurally generated dungeons where you have to delve through a dead man’s memories in order to find his soul.

Create synergies between your team, become capable of overcoming increasingly difficult enemies, reap the rewards and perfect your dream-dwelling team.

SOCIALSTEAM

More

Venture solo into the depths of the human mind with characters themed around abstract human concepts such as ideologies, life values, and dreams. You will get to explore fragments of a man's memories that have been twisted into hostile, surreal dungeons by an outside force.

These adventures into the memory moiré entail:

  • Ability upgrades and team customisation
  • A variety of enemies and special attacks
  • A lush watercolour and hand-animated world
